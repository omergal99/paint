Good news from research first: the Clipboard image API (canvas.toBlob() → ClipboardItem → navigator.clipboard.write()) is now Baseline 2024 — meaning it works reliably across all current major browsers, not just Chrome. So your Ctrl+C "copy selection as real image" requirement is very doable, no hacks needed. Paste works the same way in reverse via navigator.clipboard.read().
